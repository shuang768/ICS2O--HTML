Pancake

Pancake is a fast easy breakfast food that everyone can do.
Most people like it because of it's combination with all
sort of topings. They can have maple syrup, fruit,
butter or anything you want.

Ingredient

ingredient quantity
baking powder 3½teaspoons
salt ¼teaspoon
milk 1¼cups
melted butter 3 tablespoons

Step 1: In a large bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, salt and sugar.
Step 2: Make a well in the center and pour in the milk, egg and melted butter.
Step 3: Mix until smooth.
Step 4: Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat.
Step 5: Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake.
Step 6: Brown on both sides and serve hot.
